Remarks

Longevity and Endurance: Since its serialization began in 1997, "One Piece" has maintained its position as one of the most successful and longest-running manga and anime series of all time. Its enduring popularity speaks to its captivating story, compelling characters, and rich world-building.

Influence on Shonen Genre: As a quintessential shonen (young boys') manga, "One Piece" has had a significant impact on the genre. It has inspired numerous other manga and anime series, influencing storytelling, character development, and art styles in the medium.
